# KeywordOrbit

**Desktop keyword research at scale — Windows & Mac.**

KeywordOrbit turns a single seed keyword into tens of thousands of real long-tail queries from Google Autocomplete, enriches them with search volume, CPC, and 24-month trends from Google Keyword Planner, and clusters the results into page-ready topic groups — all from a native desktop app that keeps your data on your machine.

## Features

- **Unlimited expansion** — no daily caps or request limits
- **Free search volume** — connect your own Google Ads account to pull monthly volume, CPC, and trend history through Keyword Planner at zero marginal cost
- **One-click clustering** — tens of thousands of keywords become topic groups in seconds; each group shows total monthly demand
- **Built-in Trends** — right-click any keyword for Google Trends data, top countries, and related queries
- **Real desktop app** — projects save as local files; your research stays on your machine
