# KeywordOrbit

**Desktop keyword research at scale — Windows & Mac.**

KeywordOrbit expands a single seed keyword into thousands of long-tail keywords pulled from Google Autocomplete, enriches them with real search volume, CPC, and 24-month trends, and groups them into topic clusters — ready to export for SEO, PPC, or AI content workflows.
